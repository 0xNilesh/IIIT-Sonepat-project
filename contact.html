How to reach IIIT Sonepat?

The simplest way to reach IIT Delhi Techno Park, Sonepat is by bus. It takes around one hour to reach Rai. The bus drops you at Rai bus stand. It takes another 15-20 minutes by auto to reach IIT Delhi Techno Park. The executive way to reaching IIT Delhi Techno Park, Sonepat will be hiring a taxi.
